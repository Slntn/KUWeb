We believe the kitchen is the heart of the home and each one should be as unique as the people who live there. That's why we offer cabinets that fit every style and budget

STOCK STANDARD SIZE CABINETS


Want to get a beautiful kitchen fast, but don’t want to spend a fortune. Stock standard size cabinets will be the best fit for you. We can offer a huge variety of door styles, colors and finishes. And with our designer’s help you can expect getting the best result for your money. Brands that we carry features wood construction, superior hardware, and durable paints and finishes. EPA TSCA Title VI compliant. Greengard Gold certified. Waterbourne UV finishes. Stock cabinets provide a fast and easy way to give your kitchen a fresh new look. We can make it easier for you to keep your remodeling project on time and bring your vision to life.


SEMI-CUSTOM CABINETS

Getting all but only the standard features is not quite your way, not a problem. Semi-custom kitchen cabinets will be the great solution for you. Semi-custom cabinets are available in more styles, features and options than stock cabinets, allowing you to create a personalized look for your kitchen, at less expense than custom cabinets. They are also available with features such as rollout shelves, door racks and organization inserts, and can be embellished with crown molding, cabinet legs and feet to create a unique look. What color to choose from white and black options only? It is not the question for you anymore. We are offering spray paint services to match any color you like.


CUSTOM CABINETS

Have an unusual kitchen layout or an unconventional floor plan? Custom cabinet doors and drawers can be built to any size you specify, which makes all of your spaces usable, taking advantage of nearly every inch in your kitchen. Custom cabinets provide you with a quality personal customized selection of wood, style, finish, door styles, and hardware to address your goals and needs. And do not forget about the major benefit - custom cabinets are made by skilled cabinet makers by hand – not on assembly lines. Custom cabinets are quality construction that’s built to last.

About Us

 Kitchen Update is a family owned and operated company. It was born as a collaboration of two existing companies: Good Painter LLC, who specializes in cabinet painting and refacing; and Great Décor LLC, who specializes in kitchen remodeling. Kitchen Update is a tradename of Great Décor LLC.

 We created Kitchen Update company to provide our customers with a wide range of services to get a new look of their kitchens. We offer professional cabinet painting. Our unique process allows you to get a fresh look with maximum quality and without remodeling process hassle.

 If you don’t like the look of your existing doors, we can easily help you by replacing them with new solid wood doors in wide range of styles.

 If you feel that you are so tired of your old, useless, unattractive kitchen, that even a fresh coat of paint cannot change it, then it’s time for new cabinets. At Kitchen Update you can find 13 kitchen cabinet brands, over 100 door styles and finishes; standard size, semi-custom and custom cabinets for any taste.

 With our designer’s help and our team craftmanship you will get one of a kind custom built-in. We provide storage designs for: wine cellars, pantries, mudroom units, laundry room storage, craft room organization and more.

 Kitchen Update is licensed and insured company. We service Baltimore County, Baltimore City, Howard County, Frederick County, Montgomery County, Fairfax County, Washington DC, Alexandria City County, Arlington County.
